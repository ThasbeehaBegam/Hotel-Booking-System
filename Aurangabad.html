Tourist Places Near Aurangabad

Aurangabad is a historical city along with its surrounding towns and villages.Some tourist attractions are Ellora and Ajanta Caves, Kachner Jain Temple, Daulatabad Fort, Bibi Ka Maqbara which is an imitation of the Taj Mahal at Agra and due to its similar design, it is popularly known as the "Taj of the Deccan" etc.
