Explore Ashtamudi!

Ashtamudi Lake is the most visited backwater and lake in the state.The lake is also called the gateway to the backwaters of Kerala and is well known for its houseboat and backwater resorts. Luxury houseboats also operate on the lake. The boat journey is an 8-hour trip, winding through lakes, canals and water bound villages.
